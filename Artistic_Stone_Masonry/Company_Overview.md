# Company Profile: Artistic Stone Masonry & Supply, Inc.

## Summary
- **Established**: 1998
- **Location**: Lindon, Utah (Serving Utah, Salt Lake, Wasatch, Summit, and Weber counties).
- **Core Identity**: A family-owned, high-end custom masonry contractor and natural stone supplier specializing in the intersection of architectural precision and artisanal craftsmanship.

## Business Segments
### 1. Architectural Stone & Custom Fabrication
- **CAD Integration**: Translating architectural visions into precise stone elements using Computer-Aided Design.
- **Specialized Products**: Custom-cut moldings, wall caps, balustrades, structural columns, and bespoke stone sinks.
- **Hand-Carving**: Artistic, high-fidelity stone carving for unique residential features.

### 2. Building Stone & Veneer
- **Building Stone**: Traditional 4–5" thick quarried stone for high-durability, structural-aesthetic builds.
- **Thin Stone (Veneer)**: Decorative natural stone cut to ~1" thickness for interior/exterior siding, chimneys, and fireplaces.
- **Reclaimed Materials**: Specialized use of reclaimed brick for historical or rustic projects.

### 3. Paving & Hardscapes
- **Natural Stone Pavers**: Premium exterior flooring for driveways, walkways, and patios.
- **Complexity**: Handling large-scale residential paving projects that require precise leveling and layout.

### 4. Interior Specialties
- **Fireplaces & Hearths**: Custom-designed stone mantels and firebrick installations.
- **Sinks & Accents**: Bespoke hand-carved stone sinks and interior masonry accents.

## Operational Workflow
### 1. Procurement & Material Flow
- **Inventory Model**: Just-In-Time (JIT) procurement. Material is primarily ordered in specific quantities required to fulfill active contracts, minimizing on-site/shop inventory overhead.
- **Material Tracking**: Focus on procurement costs and material allocation per job rather than static stock levels.

### 2. Project Lifecycle
- **Bid Phase**: Initial estimation and proposal based on architectural plans or client consultations.
- **Contract Phase**: Formal agreement and project activation following a successful bid.

### 3. Cost Centers & Labor Tracking
- **Fabrication (Shop)**: Dedicated tracking of labor and resources used during the in-house stone cutting, carving, and CAD design phases.
- **Installation (Site)**: Separate tracking of labor and resources utilized during the physical build and masonry at the residential project location.

## Target Market & Collaboration
- **Primary Market**: High-end residential properties.
- **Professional Partners**: Direct collaboration with Architects, Designers, and General Contractors.
- **Value Proposition**: Exceeding standard residential expectations through innovative, industrious, and highly detailed craftsmanship.
