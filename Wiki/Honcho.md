## Summary
- **Honcho** is a platform for **Dialectic User Modeling**, used to build deep, persistent profiles of user identity and preferences.
- Enables **[[Hermes Agent]]** to maintain context and personalization across multiple sessions.
- Facilitates the transition from "General Assistant" to "Personalized Agent."
