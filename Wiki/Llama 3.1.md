## Summary
- **Llama 3.1** is a family of open-weights LLMs by Meta, ranging from **8B** to **405B** parameters.
- Built on a **Dense** architecture with significant improvements in reasoning and tool use.
- **Multilingual Support:** Supports over 8 languages natively.

## Key Models
- **Llama 3.1 8B:** Optimized for edge and local fine-tuning using **PEFT (LoRA)**.
- **Llama 3.1 70B:** Balanced performance and memory footprint.
- **Llama 3.1 405B:** State-of-the-art flagship model for complex reasoning and distillation.

## Technical Specs
- **Context Window:** Up to **128K** tokens.
- **Training Data:** 15 trillion tokens.
- **Quantization:** Compatible with **GGUF**, **EXL2**, and **GPTQ** for VRAM efficiency.
