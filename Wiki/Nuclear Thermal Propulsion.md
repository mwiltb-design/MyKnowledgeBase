## Summary
- **Mars-Forward**: NTP is identified as a critical path for future crewed missions to Mars.
- **Technical Focus**: Priority is given to developing high-temperature fuels and materials capable of withstanding extreme thermal cycles.
- **Instrumentation**: Development of radiation-hardened control systems and sensors to enable safe propulsion management.

## NTP vs NEP Funding
While both are critical, NSTM-3 establishes clear funding priorities:
- **Immediate NEP Focus**: Funding allocated to NTP will prioritize components that are cross-compatible with **Nuclear Electric Propulsion (NEP)** systems, such as shielding and reactor control.
- **Path to Mars**: NTP research is geared toward laying the groundwork for high-thrust, high-efficiency transit systems required for crewed interplanetary travel.

## Research Areas
- **Advanced Shielding**: Protecting crew and sensitive electronics from high-output reactor environments.
- **Thermal Materials**: Developing the metallurgy required for high-specific-impulse (Isp) engines.
