## Summary
- **Google Business Profile (GBP)**: The highest ROI "Fast Win" for discovery; requires 100% field completion.
- **Landmark Indexing**: Dedicated pages for every attraction/landmark within a 30-minute radius.
- **Financial Impact**: Shifting **15%** of bookings from OTAs to direct can save **$4,500–$6,750** annually per $300k revenue.

## GBP Optimization
- **Category Strategy**: Primary: "Campground"; Secondary: "RV Park" or "Cabin Rental."
- **Amenities as Services**: List "Full Hookups," "Dog Park," etc., as specific **Services** within the profile to capture long-tail search.
- **Review Management**: Automate requests post-checkout. Respond to all reviews using keyword-rich replies (mentioning site types).

## Technical SEO
- **Schema Markup**: Mandatory **LocalBusiness** and **Campground** JSON-LD for star ratings and price ranges in SERPs.
- **Proximity Marketing**: Building "RV Camping near [National Park]" landing pages to capture high-intent travelers.
- **Visual CTR**: Listings with high-quality photos receive **35% more clicks**.
