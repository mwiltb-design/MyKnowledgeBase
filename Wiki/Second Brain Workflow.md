# Second Brain Workflow

## Summary
A breakdown of the AI-driven three-folder architecture for managing knowledge through systematic raw input, wiki synthesis, and recursive self-improvement.

- System: Simple, AI-driven structure (**[[Andrej Karpathy|Karpathy's]]** principles).
- Three-Folder Architecture:
    - **Raw**: Input for notes, links, screenshots, and PDFs.
    - **Wiki**: Organized, AI-processed knowledge substrate.
    - **Archive**: Storage for processed raw source materials.
- Controller: **`Agent.md`** or **`Commands.md`** instructions for the AI agent.
- Workflow: Scan Raw -> Extract/Format -> Move to Archive.
- **Recursive Self-Improvement**: The "final boss" of knowledge management. The AI uses the Wiki to identify gaps and optimize its own research protocols.
- **[[AutoResearch]]**: Integration of experiment loops for continuous growth.
