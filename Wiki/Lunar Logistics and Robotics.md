## Summary
- **CLPS Scaling**: Increasing Commercial Lunar Payload Services (CLPS) capacity from **~4,000 kg** (Phase 1) to **~150,000 kg** (Phase 3).
- **Autonomous Manipulation**: Deploying robotic systems for unloading, repositioning, and mating/demating surface assets during uncrewed periods.
- **ISRU Integration**: In-situ resource utilization (ISRU) focused on extracting volatiles from permanently shadowed regions (PSRs).

## Logistics Framework
- **Cargo Delivery**: Utilizing a fleet of robotic helicopters and rovers for regional reconnaissance and item transfer.
- **Payload Manipulation**: Automated systems to manage resupply of key resources (water, gases) and food resupply for crewed missions.
- **Mobility Systems**: Deployment of small utility rovers and hoppers for science and resource scouting at speeds up to **10 km/hr**.

## Technical Gaps
- **Landing Accuracy**: Developing precision landing systems capable of high-accuracy range and velocity measurements in low-visibility terrain.
- **Dust Tolerance**: Mitigating the impact of lunar dust on mechanical mating systems and electronics.
