## Summary
- **Operational Automation**: Contactless kiosks and mobile check-ins to mitigate staffing shortages.
- **High-Yield Infrastructure**: Shifting focus to **Cabins** and **Glamping** as the primary revenue generators.
- **Dynamic Pricing**: Centered on **RevPAS** (Revenue Per Available Site) and lead-time benchmarking.

## Operational Efficiency
- **Contactless Systems**: Self-service kiosks handle routine check-ins, freeing staff for high-touch hospitality.
- **Automated Communication**: Pre-stay SMS/Email workflows for safety info and arrival instructions.
- **Data Syncing**: Real-time synchronization between CMS and reservation software (e.g., RoverPass, NewBook).

## Revenue Strategy
- **RevPAS Tracking**: Moving from flat rates to dynamic models that adjust based on demand and lead time.
- **Experience-Driven model**: Catering to "Digital Natives" who prioritize aesthetics and reliable connectivity.
