## Summary
- **Phased Implementation**: A three-stage approach (Phase 1: Foundation, Phase 2: Establishment, Phase 3: Endurance) to achieve sustained lunar presence.
- **Location**: Situated in the **Lunar South Pole** region for scientific and economic development.
- **Mars-Forward**: The Moon Base serves as a proving ground for crewed Mars missions, testing long-duration habitation and surface operations.

## Architecture Phasing
The Moon Base development is structured to balance near-term demonstrations with long-term infrastructure:
- **Phase 01**: Achieve high-rate, reliable surface access. Establish ground truth for landing sites.
- **Phase 02**: Establish initial lunar surface infrastructure. Semi-annual crewed missions begin.
- **Phase 03**: Transition to **Enduring Presence**. Continuous crew presence and large-scale regolith manipulation.

## Strategic Enablers
- **National Initiative**: Driven by the **[[Space Nuclear Initiative]]** (NSTM-3), establishing a goal for lunar surface reactors by 2030.
- **Commercial Innovators**: Leveraging the **CLPS** (Commercial Lunar Payload Services) program for logistics.
- **Interoperability**: Standardized interfaces for power, docking, and communications to foster a multi-provider ecosystem.
- **International Partnerships**: Collaborative development to ensure robustness and shared economic benefit.
