## Summary
- **Experience-Driven Baselines**: Modern infrastructure now requires **Pickleball Courts** (31%) and **EV Charging** (32%).
- **Connectivity as Utility**: High-performance Wi-Fi is now a non-negotiable utility, not an amenity.
- **Sustainability**: Solar power and water conservation are becoming key marketing levers for travelers.

## Physical Requirements
- **EV Infrastructure**: 32% of parks now offer charging stations; essential for the 2026 traveler segment.
- **Recreation**: High demand for social sports (Pickleball) and "Instagrammable" spots for User-Generated Content (UGC).
- **Utility Specs**: Leading with **30/50 Amp** availability and rig-specific clearances in all technical documentation.
