# RV Park Outlook 2026: Technical Trends

## Architecture & Design (Physical & Digital)
- **Experience-Driven Infrastructure:** Shift toward catering to "Digital Natives" (Millennials/Gen Z). Mandatory amenities now include **EV Charging Stations** (offered by 32% of parks) and **Pickleball Courts** (added by 31%).
- **High-Yield Units:** Prioritize construction of cabins and glamping tents; these remain the highest revenue-generators.
- **Mobile-First Architecture:** 64% of campers book directly via mobile. Sites must be "thumb-friendly" with a persistent "Book Now" button.

## Key Features
- **Operational Automation:** Contactless systems (self-service kiosks, mobile check-ins) to address staffing shortages.
- **Dynamic Pricing Tools:** Focus on **RevPAS (Revenue Per Available Site)**—benchmarking rates against booking lead times to protect margins.
- **Reliable Connectivity:** High-performance Wi-Fi is now a utility, not an amenity.

## Integration & SEO
- **Granular Page Structure:** Use dedicated landing pages for each accommodation type (e.g., "Pull-through RV Sites," "Luxury Cabins") and major amenities to improve search indexing.
- **Eco-Friendly Integration:** Sustainable practices (solar, water conservation) are increasingly valued by the 2026 traveler.
