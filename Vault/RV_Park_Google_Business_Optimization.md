# RV Park Google Business Optimization: Technical Guide

## Architecture & Design
- **Google Business Profile (GBP) Optimization:** The "Fastest Win" - complete every field.
- **Category Selection:** Use "Campground" as the primary category and "RV Park" or "Cabin Rental" as secondary.

## Key Features
- **Amenities as Services:** List every specific amenity (Full Hookups, Dog Park, Boat Ramp) as a "Service" within your GBP to match long-tail searches.
- **Review Lever:** Volume and recency matter more than a perfect 5.0. Automate a review request text the morning after checkout.

## Integration & SEO
- **Respond to Every Review:** Mention specific site types in your replies to boost keyword relevance.
- **Immersive Visuals:** Use high-quality photos (receive 35% more clicks).
