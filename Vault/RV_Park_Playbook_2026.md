# RV Park Playbook 2026: Technical Guide

## Architecture & Design
- **Experience-Driven Model:** Focus on the "Digital Native" (Millennials and Gen Z) who prioritize aesthetics and connectivity.
- **Physical Trends:** Incorporate pickleball courts and EV charging stations as baseline requirements.

## Key Features
- **Operational Automation:** Implement contactless systems (self-service kiosks and mobile check-ins) to solve the staffing crunch.
- **Dynamic Pricing Tools:** Benchmarking rates against booking lead times to protect margins during peak periods.

## Integration & SEO
- **Immersive Visuals:** Use 360° virtual tours to allow guests to "walk" the grounds before booking.
- **Mobile-First Booking:** Ensure architecture is "thumb-friendly" with a persistent "Book Now" button.
