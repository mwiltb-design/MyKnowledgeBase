# RV Park SEO Strategy: Technical Guide

## Architecture & Design
- **Granular Page Structure:** Use dedicated landing pages for each accommodation type (e.g., "Pull-through RV Sites," "Luxury Cabins") and major amenities to give search engines more "hooks" to index.
- **Mobile Load Times:** Ensure mobile load times are under 3 seconds.

## Key Features
- **Proximity Pages:** Build pages for every landmark, national park, or city within a 30-minute drive (e.g., "RV Camping near [National Park Name]").
- **Long-Tail Keywords:** Target hyper-specific searches like "pet-friendly RV park with boat ramp [County Name]" rather than broad terms.

## Integration & SEO
- **Technical Health:** Images must be compressed under 200KB.
- **Schema Markup:** Use (LocalBusiness and Campground) to help Google display star ratings and price ranges directly in search results.
- **Direct Bookings:** Shifting just 15% of bookings from OTAs (like Hipcamp or The Dyrt) to direct bookings can save $4,500–$6,750 annually in commissions for a $300k/year park.
