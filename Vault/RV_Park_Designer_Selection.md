# RV Park Website Designer Selection: Technical Guide

## Architecture & Design
- **Hierarchy:** Prioritizes **"Fit First, Amenities Second."** Technical layouts must lead with physical site constraints (rig length, turning radius, slide-out clearance) to reduce bounce rates from "big rig" owners.
- **Platform Strategy:** Recommends a **decoupled architecture**. Use a high-performance marketing site (WordPress/Squarespace) for content, linked via API or direct deep-link to a specialized booking engine (Campspot, ResNexus). Avoid the "free" but limited sub-domain sites provided by software vendors.

## Key Features
- **Utility Specs:** Mandatory inclusion of **30/50 amp service** and clear "Pull-through vs. Back-in" designations.
- **Site Fidelity:** High-resolution photos of *actual* pads and hookups rather than stock photography.

## Integration & SEO
- **Search Intent:** Align content with specific traveler needs (e.g., "Big Rig Friendly" or "Full Hookups").
- **Booking Flow:** Minimize clicks from the landing page to the reservation grid.
