# RV Park Digital Presence Guide: Technical Overview

## Architecture & Design
- **Experience-Driven Model:** Focus on the "Digital Native" (Millennials and Gen Z) who prioritize aesthetics and connectivity.
- **Mobile-First Architecture:** 64% of campers book directly via mobile. Sites must be "thumb-friendly" with a persistent "Book Now" button.

## Key Features
- **Granular Page Structure:** Use dedicated landing pages for each accommodation type (e.g., "Pull-through RV Sites," "Luxury Cabins") and major amenities to give search engines more "hooks" to index.
- **Operational Automation:** Contactless systems (self-service kiosks and mobile check-ins) to address the staffing shortage.

## Integration & SEO
- **Immersive Visuals:** 360° virtual tours to allow guests to "walk" the grounds before booking.
- **UGC (User-Generated Content):** 84% of people trust guest photos more than ads. Create "Instagrammable" spots in your park and encourage guests to tag your location to generate free, high-trust marketing.
